01 - Lense Of JG

See Live Site

Client: Jared Garcia (Lense of JG)

Project Proposal: See Proposal

Technologies: HTML, CSS, SCSS, JavaScript Bootstrap, Git, Netlify, Vite, Photoshop, XD

Project Description: The client, Lense Of JG needed, a responsive website where they could showcase their different services offered as am up and coming media company. The project was orginally supposed to showcase more of their video work but as the project was underway the client decided they wanted to shift to showcase their photography services more until further notice. They still wanted to represent their videography and video editing services without having entire pages dedicated to it.

Design: Part of the challenge for this project was shifting gears as I had already begun to code the site according to the initial wireframe. This required to me to rework design elements to showcase photography galleries as a primary and some videography as a secondary purpose of the site.

Another unique challenge for this website was having different video clips to fit the responsive nature of the site. Becasue a normal video clip is shot landscape, I needed to also provide the mobile version of the site with vertical videos, that was accomplished by changing the videos using Bootstrap breakpoints and SASS

Development: To give the gallery website a more artisic feel I devided to incorporate small animations via Javascript to create fade-in effects as well as a horizontal scroll that only requires the user to scroll on the page and not click and drag over.
